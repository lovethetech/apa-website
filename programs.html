Programs

Programs that celebrate culture and strengthen community.

APA delivers year-round programming for families, youth, elders, and community partners. Each initiative connects people to culture, education, and community care.

Signature Programs

Explore the initiatives that define APA. Programs are adapted each season to respond to community needs.

Language

Punjabi Language Labs

Weekly sessions focused on conversational Punjabi, literacy, and cultural storytelling for all ages.

Arts

Bhangra and Folk Arts

Dance, music, and visual arts workshops celebrating Punjabi heritage and creative expression.

Youth

Youth Leadership Circles

Mentorship, volunteering, and leadership development for youth across Alberta.

Wellness

Community Wellness Series

Mental wellness talks, health education, and family support sessions led by trusted partners.

Support

Newcomer Support Hub

Settlement resources, career coaching, and community navigators for new Albertans.

Community

Seva and Service Days

Volunteer-led service days supporting food security and community wellbeing.

How We Design Programs

Programs are co-created with community members, youth leaders, and elders. We focus on cultural authenticity, inclusive access, and measurable impact.
