About APA

A community rooted in heritage and built for the future.

Alberta Punjabi Association brings together families, elders, youth, and community partners to celebrate Punjabi culture and provide meaningful community care across the province.

Our Story

Registered in 2026, APA was formed to ensure Punjabi heritage thrives in Alberta. We create spaces where elders share stories, youth grow as leaders, and newcomers feel supported from their first days in Canada.

With volunteer leadership and community partnerships, APA continues to expand cultural events, language programs, and wellness supports.

What We Believe

Culture connects us across generations. Community care is everyone’s responsibility. Belonging fuels confidence and leadership.

Values in Action

Every APA program is guided by the values that shape Punjabi culture: seva (service), respect, and collective joy.

Seva and Service

Community care drives our outreach, food drives, and wellness programming.
